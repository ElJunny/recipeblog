First get the following items from local doggo market: Rice Kripy, Choclate Chip, Royal Icing, and finally a doggo... For guideness

Assembling >:3

Alrighty seeing as how you got the ingredients for making the perfect egg dogo crisspy let's begin shall we.

  • Step 1:First assemble 2 rice kirspies and mush them together until they form a perfect circle, then form into an oval like shape leaving the bottom flat
  • Step 2: Once done rolling the krispies into a spehere like oval. Lay the Royal icing around the krispy then give leftover to doggo, if any.
  • Step 3: Final step, get your choclate chips from bag and stick pointy side to the crispy and smush them together, rember try not to squish too hard as it may lead to messy horrifying eyes that may lead to doggo parking it's lambo in your toilet. That's it you've made a rice krispy doggo edition! Hope you and your dogo enjoy a delicious treat.
